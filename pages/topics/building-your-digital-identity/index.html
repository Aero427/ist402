Theory

A theory I learned about in Grad school that stuck with me the most is called Actor Network Theory (ANT). ANT can be argued as to if it's actually a theory, but the TLDR; is that all things influence each other.

For information sciences though, it's saying that anything created influences anything else created. This is particularly potent in the digital space when we can create a logo, brand, business, website, identity, or platform seemingly out of thin air. If I post on YouTube, I'm not just broadcasting there and increasing my brand recognition in that space. It's aggregated to blogs, it shows up in Google and Bing search results. Its lifted for replication on Russian botnet sites.

All of this, amplifies your voice and reach far beyond you speaking in public (again, duh).
